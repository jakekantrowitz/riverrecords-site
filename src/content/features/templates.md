Stream’s template system gives you control without friction.

You can design templates for any part of the documentation process: 

• **Subjective** – customize how patient narratives appear, whether bullet-pointed symptoms or structured HPI subsections.

• **Objective** – capture exams, vitals, labs, or imaging details with reusable formatting.

• **Assessment & Plan** – standardize your reasoning, differential language, and management plans for consistency across visits.  

For clinicians who prefer problem-oriented documentation, Stream also supports full **problem-based templates**—SOAP cards written entirely about one medical issue. Each card can inherit its own structure and style.

You can move seamlessly along the spectrum—from fully problem-based notes (SOAP per problem) to encounter-based notes (S/O/A grouped by visit). Even in encounter mode, Stream can still apply **problem-specific mini-templates**, ensuring consistent organization and carry-forward structure.

Templates are reusable, editable, and stored securely inside your workspace. Once you’ve created one, simply select it—or let Stream auto-apply it based on visit type or specialty context.

The result: documentation that’s faster, cleaner, and consistently reflects how *you* think.
