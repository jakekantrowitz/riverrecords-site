Solo practitioners shoulder the immense responsibility of managing their entire practice—from patient care to administrative tasks—without the luxury of a larger team for support. While rewarding, this balancing act can be overwhelming, with tasks like documentation, chart review, and compliance often eating into precious time that could be spent with patients.

River Records’ Stream, an AI-powered documentation solution, is here to help. Designed specifically for busy clinicians, Stream offers a hands-free, efficient way to manage documentation, freeing solo practitioners to focus on what matters most: providing exceptional patient care.

 

## Challenges of Solo Practice

Operating a solo practice means wearing many hats, often simultaneously. Documentation alone can consume hours every day, leaving less time for patient interaction and personal downtime. Key challenges include:

- **Administrative Overload**: Managing patient notes, chart updates, and follow-ups.
- **Distraction from Patient Care**: Balancing focus between patient conversations and note-taking.
- **Time Management**: Finding time for both quality care and business management tasks.

These challenges can lead to burnout, reduce patient satisfaction, and make it difficult to maintain a thriving practice.

 

 

## Introducing Stream: AI-Powered Documentation for Solo Clinicians

**Stream** revolutionizes the documentation process by automating routine tasks and enhancing efficiency. Unlike traditional methods that disrupt patient interactions, Stream passively supports clinicians by capturing and organizing data in real-time.

**What Does Stream Do?**

- **Automated Documentation
**Stream listens during patient visits, capturing essential information without interrupting the natural flow of conversation. It organizes the data into problem-based cards, reflecting a clinician’s thought process.
- **Real-Time Note Generation
**Customizable notes are generated within moments after a visit concludes, reducing the time spent on charting and ensuring accuracy.
- **EHR Integration
**With a single click, Stream transfers notes, problem summaries, and ICD codes directly to your Electronic Health Record (EHR), streamlining the process further.
- **Multi-Language Support
**Stream supports over 20 languages, including English, Spanish, and French, making it accessible to diverse patient populations.
- **Hands-Free Operation
**A simple click to start recording means clinicians can focus entirely on patient interactions without the distraction of typing or screen time.
