## Understanding Automated Clinical Documentation

Automated clinical documentation is the process of capturing and managing patient information using advanced technology such as artificial intelligence (AI). These systems streamline administrative workflows, reduce errors, and enable healthcare providers to focus on delivering quality care.

**Stream by River Records** takes this to the next level by:

- Capturing patient interactions in **real-time**, allowing clinicians to remain fully engaged with their patients.
- Organizing information by **medical problems**, ensuring records are accessible and actionable.
- Integrating seamlessly with **electronic health records (EHRs)**, enhancing efficiency and connectivity across systems.

 

## The Importance of Longitudinal Care

Longitudinal care is a comprehensive approach to managing a patient’s health journey over time. It fosters continuity, strengthens patient-provider relationships, and ensures improved health outcomes. However, achieving longitudinal care hinges on accurate and accessible clinical documentation.

With Stream’s advanced capabilities, healthcare teams can:

- Access a **complete history** of patient interactions, ensuring continuity of care.
- Reduce administrative tasks, freeing up providers to focus on treatment and care coordination.
- Enhance collaboration across multidisciplinary teams by providing clear and organized patient data.
