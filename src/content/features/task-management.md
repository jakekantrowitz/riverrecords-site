In clinical care, the hard part isn’t always deciding what to do — it’s making sure it actually gets done. Between follow-up steps, administrative tasks, care coordination, and the realities of visit-to-visit documentation, important clinical details can easily slip through the cracks.

**Stream’s Task Management feature eliminates that cognitive burden.**

### Stream listens and identifies actionable items automatically

Whether you’re speaking naturally, typing into the Scratchpad, or documenting through templates, Stream identifies and surfaces the next steps implied by your visit — follow-ups to schedule, instructions to carry out, reminders to address at the next visit, or actions to complete outside the room.

There’s no setup required. No triggers to define. No special syntax.

If you say it, Stream captures it.

### A unified task list for your practice

Stream gathers all identified tasks in a dedicated Task Manager page, filterable by provider. This gives your entire practice a single source of truth for clinical and administrative follow-ups — a view that’s far easier to manage than a mix of sticky notes, inbox messages, or EHR reminders.

### Tasks carry forward until you complete them

Every task persists across encounters until you mark it resolved. If you don’t complete the task today, it will still appear the next time you see the patient.

This reduces the mental overhead of remembering what needs to happen at future visits.

It also prevents important gaps in follow-up care.

### Simple resolution, clean workflows

Tasks are manually resolved with a single action. No automatic clearing, no hidden logic — you maintain full control.

### Designed for Stream Pro

Tasks depend on Stream’s longitudinal memory, context loading, and continuity model. Because of this, Task Management is available exclusively in **Stream Pro**.
