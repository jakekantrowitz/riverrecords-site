Every clinician knows the feeling — a new patient arrives with years of records from outside systems: scanned PDFs, faxed letters, printouts from old EHRs. You spend half the visit scrolling, searching, and summarizing before you can even begin to plan care.

Stream’s **Record Ingestion** feature is designed to fix that.

Simply upload a PDF or paste text from any outside record, and Stream automatically extracts the relevant information — problems, medications, key findings — and files each item into the right **problem buckets** for you.

Discharge summaries, referral letters, and old consult notes become structured, problem-linked documentation that you can actually use. The next time you see the patient, you’re not digging through attachments — you’re continuing the story from where someone else left off.

This feature will also form the foundation of Stream’s **Chart Builder**, allowing you to reconstruct a patient’s longitudinal record even when they come from multiple external systems.

And soon, you’ll even be able to **fax directly into Stream** — sending outside documents straight into the problem-based charting system for instant processing. (We’re gauging early interest in this — stay tuned.)

With Record Ingestion, Stream turns information overload into structured, usable context — bringing clarity back to every handoff, every referral, and every new patient you meet.
