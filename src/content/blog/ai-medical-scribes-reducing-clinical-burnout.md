## Understanding Clinical Burnout in Modern Healthcare

Clinical burnout affects a significant percentage of healthcare professionals, with symptoms including exhaustion, stress, and a sense of reduced efficacy. Factors contributing to burnout range from long hours to high-stakes decision-making; however, one of the most addressable drivers is the overwhelming amount of time spent on documentation. Physicians often face hours of charting, reporting, and data entry, leaving them with less time for patient interaction, complex case analysis, and continuity of care.
