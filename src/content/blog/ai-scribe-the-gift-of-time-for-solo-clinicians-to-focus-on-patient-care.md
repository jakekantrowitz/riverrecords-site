## The Documentation Dilemma for Solo Clinicians

Documentation is a cornerstone of healthcare, ensuring patient safety, compliance, and continuity of care. However, for solo practitioners, it often becomes a time-consuming burden. Studies have shown that healthcare providers spend up to two hours on documentation for every hour of direct patient care. This imbalance not only contributes to burnout but also reduces the quality time clinicians can devote to their patients. As patient volumes increase, particularly during seasonal surges, the need for a streamlined documentation process becomes even more critical.
