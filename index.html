How it works

Every feature designed to save minutes now and hours later.

Stream combines a real-time AI scribe with a problem-oriented chart that builds longitudinal context across every encounter.

Step 01

Speak naturally. Stream generates the note.

Record your encounter or dictate a quick update — Stream transcribes, structures, and writes a complete note in real time. Your voice sets the context; Stream does the rest.

Supports 22 languages. Works ambient or with direct dictation. Customizable templates for your specialty.

Recording in progress
Live Transcript
↓ Generating SOAP note…
Step 02

Every visit organized by medical problem — not by date.

Stream doesn't just create a note. It updates your patient's problem list with each encounter, connecting history, assessments, and plans per condition.

No more re-reading six past notes to find when the metformin dose changed. It's right there, under the problem.

Problem List
Step 03

Tasks, coding, and admin handled automatically.

Follow-up tasks are generated from every note. ICD and CPT codes are suggested at point of care. External records are imported, summarized, and linked to the relevant problem.

From referral letters to prior auth — Stream reduces the busywork so you can focus on what matters.
